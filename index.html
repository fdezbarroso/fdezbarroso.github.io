Lilith

Master's Degree Thesis Project - Unreal Engine 5

An action roguelike videogame in which you corrupt the map, built as part of a large multidisciplinary team, consisting of over 20 people.

In it I took ownership of many features, helping with others as needed, including:
Setup of the Perforce depot and Jenkins jobs and scripts, the code's structure, with a Gameplay Ability Systems and event-based architecture, enemy spawning and pathfinding, the Dash, Gluttony and Greed abilities, the application of corruption effects, the progression system, UI, and sound, along with the FMOD integration.
Still in development.
